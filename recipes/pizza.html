Margherita Pizza

Home

Description:

A margherita pizza is a classic Italian pizza known for its simple yet high-quality ingredients that represent the colors of the Italian flag. Originating in Naples, it features a thin, hand-kneaded crust, often baked in a wood-fired oven. The toppings consist of vibrant red San Marzano tomato sauce, fresh white mozzarella cheese, and aromatic green basil leaves. A final drizzle of extra-virgin olive oil brings all the fresh, tangy, and creamy flavors together in a harmonious and authentic Italian delight.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Recipe Pizza Dough
  • 1/2 cup Pizza Sauce
  • 2 cups Fresh Sliced Mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • Fresh Basil Leaves

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 475F. Prepare all ingredients for the margherita pizza.
  2. On a floured surface, roll the dough into the desired shape. Top it off with pizza sauce, pieces of fresh mozzarella and sprinkle a bit of parmesan cheese.
  3. Bake pizza for about 14 minutes or until the crust is lightly browned and the cheese is golden.
  4. Remove pizza from the oven, top it off with fresh basil. Serve pizza right away, while the cheese is all melted.
